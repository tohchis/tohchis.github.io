Impact on the Community

Description

Sky Tavern gets 10 new ski racks to replace their older ones for their students to use. These ski racks have added benefits. They are powder coated, easier to move than their older ski racks, and have enough spacing to fit snowboards. They are more efficient, sturdy, and reliable for the sake that Sky Tavern won't have to worry about their ski racks falling apart.
