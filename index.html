About Us

Our Organization

Ski Racks for Sky Tavern is the final year of a legacy Junior Service Learning project.
This organization is led by 3 highschool students from the career technical school Academy of Arts, Careers, and Technology.
We fabricated essential equipment to donate to the nonprofit Sky Tavern using skills learned from school, our goal was 10 ski racks.
To fund our project, we reached out within the community for donations.

Jonathan Ramos

Jonathan is our JSL project leader and one of two welders. He has taken 3 years of welding at the Truckee Meadows Community College.

John Aguila

John is also a welder and has also taken 3 years of welding at TMCC. Jonathan and John are able to take these welding classes for dual credits (high school and college) from AACT.

Enrico Arvisu

Enrico went down the manufacturing branch of AACT in his sophomore year. He has taken two years of welding, and two years of manufacturing. He created this website.
